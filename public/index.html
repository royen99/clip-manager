About ComfyUI Clip Manager

ComfyUI Clip Manager is an AI-powered localized video management system designed specifically for ComfyUI generated content.

How It Works

  • Upload your AI-generated videos
  • System automatically extracts prompts & workflow data
  • Content is analyzed and auto-tagged by vision AI
  • Search and filter your collection by prompt, tags, or model

Allowed Content

Only videos created with ComfyUI containing embedded metadata (workflow/prompts) are accepted.

Strictly Prohibited

Uploading content depicting children and/or minors (CSAM) or any other illegal content is strictly forbidden and will be rejected.
